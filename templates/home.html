Travel Home Page

Welcome to the University of Galway travel site.

This web app is designed to allow staff and students to log their travel. Staff in Global Galway will be able to monitor if a disaster or event occurs close to your travel location, so we can check in.

Here you can add, update and view your travel history. Plus see FCO advice for your or any disasters that may have occurred near to your travel locations.



If you would like to browse and search the event reports directly please go to: Relief Web disasters
